Rufaqaau-z-Zikr Academy

Who are We? We are an online academy that has recognized the struggle of the average Muslim woman when it comes to learning Islam and the Arabic language in Nigeria. Putting you and your struggles at the core of our existence, we have created this academy as a safe place for you to learn with fellow women. And in a bid to make it a safe place that identifies with you, we have only female teachers. So, we are an academy for Muslim Women by Muslim Women.

What do We Offer?

  1. Female only teachers
  2. Qur'an Recitation and Memorization
  3. Arabic Classes
  4. Hadeeth and Fiqh
  5. Weekly Discussion Forum
